The Cosmic Family

In the vast, silent theater of the solar system, Chandra, the Moon, was puzzled. He looked towards his brilliant older brother, Surya, the Sun, who had been his source of light and guidance for eons.

The solar system is our cosmic neighborhood where planets, moons, and the sun dance together in perfect harmony.

Page 2 of 7
Chandra

"Brother Surya! There's something I don't understand. On some days, a huge, dark shadow from Earth creeps over me. But I'm not scared. Instead, I find myself glowing in a wonderful coppery-red color! How is this possible?"

Chandra is experiencing a lunar eclipse, when Earth comes between the Sun and the Moon.

Page 3 of 7
Surya

"That is an eclipse... but it is so much more special than that. We call it an 'Amethyst Promise.' The Earth blocks my direct light from reaching you, but something magical happens!"

During a lunar eclipse, Earth's atmosphere filters sunlight, bending red and orange light onto the Moon, creating a beautiful red glow.

Chandra

"Me? I'm big enough to cover you? That's like a tiny lamp shade trying to cover a giant searchlight!"

Surya

"You are 400 times smaller than me, but you are also 400 times closer to Earth! This 'perfect alignment' makes you cover me perfectly. In those moments, the 'Diamond Ring' appears as my corona is revealed!"

The Moon and Sun appear almost the same size in our sky, allowing for the spectacular sight of a total solar eclipse.
